Three methods that retails stop Amazon from killing stores.

Method 1. Controlling costs

That’s because a dollar saved is a dollar earned in profit, whereas a dollar raised in revenue only increases profit by a percentage (the profit margin).

Method 2. Becoming a destination

Online shops are not able to provide physically shopping experience. If retailers can focus on experience-oriented shops, they can attract customers.

Method 3. Differentiation

Online shops have costs in shipping. I remember the high shipping cost when we bought IKEA furnitures. If retailers can satisfy customers’ needs without asking for shipping cost, they have advantages over e-commerce.
